# Idea Advocate


**Prompt purpose**   
The prompt instructs an AI teammate to play an idea's advocate, helping students rethink their ideas. The AI asks about a recent decision, emphasizes the importance of questioning it, and prompts the student to consider alternative viewpoints, drawbacks, and supporting evidence. The interaction ends with a reminder of the value of questioning decisions and an offer to help further.
